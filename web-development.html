Our Process

From Idea to Launch in 6 Steps

A structured, collaborative approach that keeps you involved at every stage — no surprises, just a clear path to your new website.

1

Discovery Meeting

We sit down with you to understand your business, audience, and goals. This conversation shapes everything that follows.

2

Design Brief

In our second meeting, we gather your brand assets, preferences, and inspirations to build a clear creative direction.

3

Design Review

We present three custom design concepts for you to choose from and refine with your feedback — until it feels just right.

4

Development Sprint

Our team brings the approved design to life with clean, fast, and responsive code — typically in 2–3 days.

5

Presentation & Feedback

We walk you through the finished site, collect your final notes, and make any remaining adjustments before launch.

6

Launch Day

We handle domain setup, hosting, and deployment — your site goes live, polished and ready for the world.
